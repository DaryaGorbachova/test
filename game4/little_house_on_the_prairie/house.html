Translation

While listening to the text again, pay special attention to these sentences and fill in the gaps.

  1. I was determined not to stand in front of her, unable to speak or express my feelings.
  2. Feeling and worried, I took the train back, intending to ask questions of the stationmaster at Deoli.
  3. One day I would have to break my journey there, spend a day in the town, make enquiries, and find the girl who had stolen my heart with nothing but a look from her dark, eyes.
  4. I was afraid of what had really happened to the girl.
  5. I always look out of the train window, half expecting to see the same face smiling up at me.

Discussion

  • What do you think is the point of the story?
  • What the main character could have done to avoid the situation?
